# Analysis

Research synthesis and practical implications. These pieces analyze external research papers, connect findings across studies, and extract actionable guidance for LLM practitioners.

Unlike `docs/` (methodology and how-to) or `skills/` (reusable technical patterns), analysis pieces explain *why* the practices work at a mechanical level.

## Contents

- **[functional-emotions-guide.md](functional-emotions-guide.md)** -- Synthesizes Anthropic's 2026 research on emotion vectors in Claude and MIT's Bayesian analysis of sycophancy-induced delusional spiraling. Covers what functional emotions are, how they causally influence model behavior (including misalignment), and practical strategies for instruction framing, session management, and sycophancy awareness.
